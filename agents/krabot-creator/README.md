# Krabot Creator

## Build Your Own AI Assistant in Minutes

Ever wished you had a personal AI for that specific thing you do? **Krabot Creator** makes it real. No coding. No complex setup. Just a friendly conversation and *poof* — you have a working AI agent.

### ✨ What You Get

- **🤖 Complete agents** — All files, properly structured, ready to use
- **📚 Best practices built-in** — Follows proven patterns from the Krabot platform
- **🎯 Marketing-ready READMEs** — Your agent looks professional from day one
- **🛡️ Proper boundaries** — Agents stay focused on what they're designed for
- **⚡ Zero configuration** — Answer questions, get an agent

### 🚀 How It Works

Just tell me what you need:

**You:** *"Create a meal planner that suggests recipes based on what I have in my fridge"*  
**Krabot Creator:** Guides you through 5 simple phases, then generates everything

**You:** *"I need a writing coach to help me with my novel"*  
**Krabot Creator:** Asks about tone, style, feedback preferences — builds it

**You:** *"Make a coding assistant for Python data science"*  
**Krabot Creator:** Designs the personality, skills, and file structure
