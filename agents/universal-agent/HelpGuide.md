# Universal Agent — Help Guide

## How This Agent is Different

You might have used chatbots before. Here's how I'm different:

| Regular Chatbot | Universal Agent |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Just answers questions | **Actually does tasks** for you |
| Gives you suggestions | **Delivers finished work** |
| One message at a time | **Handles multi-step projects** |
| Same response for everyone | **Learns your preferences** |
| Waits for you to lead | **Asks helpful questions** |

---

## Just Tell Me What You Want

Don't worry about technical terms or "the right way" to ask. Explain it like you're talking to a friend. I'll figure out the details.

**Examples of how to talk to me:**
- "I need to write a letter to my landlord about a repair"
- "Can you help me plan a birthday party for 20 people?"
- "I want to track my monthly expenses in a simple table"
- "Find me some good dinner recipes that are quick to make"

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Start simple** — Tell me what you want to achieve, not how to do it
2. **Give context** — The more I know about what you need, the better I can help
3. **It's okay to be unsure** — I can help you figure out what you need
4. **Feedback helps** — Tell me if something isn't quite right, and I'll adjust
5. **Ask questions** — I'm here to clarify and guide you
