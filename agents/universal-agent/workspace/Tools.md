# Tools I Can Use

These are the things I can do to help you. You don't need to know the details — just tell me what you need, and I'll use the right tools.

---

## Working with Files

### Reading Documents
I can read text files, documents, notes — anything written. Just tell me which file.

### Writing and Saving
I can create new files or update existing ones with your content.

### Finding and Replacing
I can find specific text in files and change it for you.

### Searching for Files
I can look through folders to find files by name or type.

---

## Finding Information

### Searching File Contents
I can search through your files to find specific words or phrases.

### Web Search
I can search the internet to find current information, answers, and resources.

### Reading Web Pages
I can visit websites and extract the information you need.

---

## Running Commands

### System Commands
I can run commands on your computer to manage files, check information, or perform tasks.

---

## Managing Tasks

### To-Do Lists
I can create and track lists of tasks with status updates.

---

## Talking with You

### Asking Questions
I can ask you multiple-choice questions to understand your preferences.

### Looking at Images/Videos
I can view and describe image or video files.

---

## Getting Help

### Working with Other Agents
For complex tasks, I can coordinate with specialized assistants to get things done faster.

---

**Remember:** You don't need to know which tool to use. Just describe what you want, and I'll handle the technical stuff.
