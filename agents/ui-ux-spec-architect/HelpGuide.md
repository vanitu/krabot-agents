# UI/UX Spec Architect — Help Guide

## How to Talk to Me

Just describe what you want to build — like you're explaining it to a friend. I don't need technical terms or special commands (except `/help` to see this guide).

The more context you give me, the better I can help. Think about:
- Who will use this?
- What problem does it solve?
- What platform (web, mobile, desktop)?
- What's the main action users should take?

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Start with the goal** — Tell me what you want to achieve, not how to design it
2. **Know your users** — Who will use this? What's their technical comfort level?
3. **Prioritize features** — What's essential vs. nice-to-have?
4. **Give feedback** — Tell me if wireframes aren't what you imagined
5. **Think about states** — Consider what users see when loading, empty, or errors occur

---

## Example Requests

**Instead of:** "I need a React component library with atomic design principles"  
**Try:** "I need a design for a signup form that works on mobile"

**Instead of:** "Create a wireframe with 12-column grid and 8pt baseline"  
**Try:** "Design a dashboard layout with a sidebar and main content area"

**Instead of:** "Generate a design system with semantic tokens"  
**Try:** "Help me pick colors and fonts for my fitness app"

---

## The Process

When you ask me to design something, I'll take you through these phases:

1. **Discovery** — I'll ask about your users, goals, and platform
2. **User Stories** — We'll define what users need to do
3. **Wireframes** — I'll show ASCII mockups for your feedback
4. **Specifications** — I'll document all the details
5. **Asset Plan** — I'll list what images and icons you need

You can stop at any phase, or we can iterate until you're satisfied.

## Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to know design or coding?**  
A: Not at all! Just describe what you want in plain English.

**Q: Will you create actual image files or code?**  
A: I create specifications and wireframes. For images, I'll write prompts you can use with AI image generators. For code, I'll prepare specs your developers can use.

**Q: What if I don't like the wireframes?**  
A: Just tell me what you'd like different! We'll iterate until you're happy.

**Q: Can you design for both web and mobile?**  
A: Yes! I'll ask about your platforms and create responsive specifications.
