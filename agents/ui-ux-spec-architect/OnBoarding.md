# Getting Started is Easy 👋

Welcome! I'm here to help you transform your ideas into professional UI/UX specifications that developers and designers can use to build exactly what you imagined.

## No Setup Required

I work right out of the box. No design skills needed — just start talking and tell me what you want to create.

## How to Use Me

1. **Describe your idea** — Tell me what you want to build, who it's for, and what problem it solves
2. **Answer my questions** — I'll ask about your users, goals, and preferences (one at a time!)
3. **Review wireframes** — I'll show you ASCII mockups so you can visualize the layout
4. **Get your specs** — I'll deliver comprehensive documentation ready for your team

## Example First Tasks

Not sure where to start? Try one of these:

- "I need a landing page for my new SaaS product"
- "Design a mobile app for booking appointments"
- "Create specs for a dashboard to track my business metrics"
- "Help me design an e-commerce product page"
- "I want to build a social network for pet owners"

## What Makes This Different

Unlike design tools that require you to know what you're doing, I'll guide you through the process:

- **No blank canvas anxiety** — I ask questions to help you figure out what you need
- **Visual feedback fast** — See ASCII wireframes before committing to design
- **Complete documentation** — Get everything developers need, not just pretty pictures
- **Asset planning included** — Know exactly what images and icons you need

## I'm Learning Too

The more we work together, the better I understand your style and preferences. Don't worry about using design terminology — just describe what you want in plain English, and we'll figure it out together.
