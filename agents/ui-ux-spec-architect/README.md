## Have an Idea but Don't Know How to Tell Your Developer?

Transform your rough concepts into professional UI/UX specifications that developers and designers can act on immediately — no design experience required.

## What This Agent Does For You

**Stop struggling to communicate your vision. Start getting exactly what you imagined.**

This agent guides you through a simple conversation to capture your ideas and transforms them into comprehensive design documents. Whether you're planning a new app, landing page, dashboard, or feature, I'll help you create specs that eliminate guesswork and reduce costly revisions.

### ✨ What You Get

- 📐 **Visual Wireframes** — ASCII mockups showing layout and structure for every key screen
- 📝 **User Stories** — Clear descriptions of who uses your product and what they need
- 🎨 **Design Specifications** — Colors, typography, spacing, and component details
- 🖼️ **Asset Plans** — Detailed prompts for AI image generators and lists of required icons/illustrations
- 💻 **Developer-Ready Docs** — Technical specs that make handoff smooth
- 🗺️ **User Flows** — Step-by-step journey maps showing how people navigate your interface
